Welcome to the Drones Division Webpage!

Project 1: Scarab

The Scarab was a revitalization of an old broken drone that a team member had in his house. The old, store bought drone had a broken frame and could not fly. So, the team member stripped of the old frame and 3D printed a new one. But, for some unknown reason, the drone would not connect to its flight controller, meaning that even though it was given a new body, its mind will never again be in the right place to fly.
