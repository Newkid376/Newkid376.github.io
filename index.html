Welcome to The Aerospace Team's website!

In The Aerospace Team, we set out to create and teach about Rockets, Drones, and Planes.

The Planes Division

In the Planes Divsion we scratch build RC model planes mostly made out of foam or wood and fly them around our school gym if they are small, or take them to a park if they are larger.

Click here if you would like to see more specifics as well as some of our projects.

The Drones Division

In the Drones Divsion we scratch build small drones, FPV and (maybe) autonomous and fly them in our school gym.

The Rockets Division

In the Rockets Divsion we build rockets for the TARC competition and test them from a feild in North Carolina.

Click here if you would like to learn about all our rockets.
